About FarmBridge

FarmBridge Technologies was founded in October 2025 with a clear mission: to build a transparent, technology-driven agricultural marketplace that connects farmers directly with buyers. By reducing unnecessary middlemen and improving price discovery, FarmBridge aims to create scalable, efficient, and fair agricultural trade infrastructure built for long-term impact and trust.
